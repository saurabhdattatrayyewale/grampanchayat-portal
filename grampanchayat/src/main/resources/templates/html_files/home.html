Welcome to Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Portal

We are committed to providing transparent, efficient, and citizen-friendly services to all residents of rural Maharashtra. Our online portal enables you to access various gram panchayat services from the comfort of your home, reducing the need for physical visits and long queues.

Key features of our portal include:

  • 24/7 access to gram panchayat services from anywhere
  • Secure online payment options with multiple gateways
  • Real-time application tracking with SMS updates
  • Transparent process with digital receipts and records
  • Access to all government schemes and benefits
  • Direct communication with panchayat officials
  • Mobile-friendly interface for easy access
