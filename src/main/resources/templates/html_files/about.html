About Rural Development & Panchayat Raj Department

Welcome to the official website of the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department, Government of Maharashtra. Established on 1st May 1960, our mission is to achieve constructive, comprehensive, and sustainable rural development through a strong Panchayati Raj system.

Our Mission

"Rural Development of the state by strengthening the Panchayati Raj System."

Our Objectives

  • Implementing initiatives to create clean, beautiful, and green villages.
  • Undertaking poverty alleviation activities through the Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Improvement Mission.
  • Providing shelter and related facilities under the Indira Awas Yojana.
  • Strengthening the Panchayati Raj system by empowering public representatives through training and development programs.

Key Initiatives

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana

Providing rural connectivity through all-weather roads to unconnected habitations.

Revamped Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan

Enhancing Panchayati Raj institutions' capacity for effective governance.

Basic Facilities Within Villages

Developing infrastructure as per local needs to improve quality of life.
